Automation ROI Calculation & Candidate Identification

Senior Specialist EMTE Metric Implementation Strategy

EMTE (Equivalent Manual Testing Effort) Formula

Senior specialist created this comprehensive metric to quantify automation ROI. I will apply it to all scripts in use and store the data in the database for future insights to make informed decisions on automation.

EMTE = (Tm - Ta) × (F × P) - (M × P)

Where:
• Tm = Time to execute manually (minutes)
• Ta = Time to execute automatically
• F = Frequency of runs in period
• P = Number of periods
• M = Maintenance overhead per period

Automation Candidate Criteria

  • Repetitive or frequent execution - High ROI potential
  • Critical accuracy requirements - Reduce human error
  • Rules-based and deterministic - Suitable for automation
  • Covers critical functionality - Maximum impact
  • Stable and predictable - Lower maintenance costs
  • Significant manual effort savings - Justified investment
